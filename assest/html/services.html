Services

I employ a method of creating art called paper quilling that incorporates elements of painting with paper, giving each artwork a 3D look, with texture and feel People stop and gaze for extended periods at this extremely rare art form The exclusivity and rarity that accompany each of my pieces are part of their distinct selling factors or values. I have close to a decade of experience working in the creative and educational sectors, offering design and instructional services to Nigerian schools.

Paper Modelling

The Art of constructing models of objects, items or characters from sheets of paper, papaerboards or cardstock.

Paper Quilling

The art of creating decorative designs from thin strips of curled paper known as "quills" rolled, shaped and glued together.
